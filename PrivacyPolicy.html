This Privacy Policy describes how Muraivazhi (“we”, “our”, “us”), developed and operated by Leafsof Technologies, collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our application or related services. By using the app, you agree to the terms outlined below.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: name, email, phone number, and profile details you provide (if applicable).
  • Technical Data: device type, app version, and limited log data for analytics and troubleshooting.

3. Microphone Permission & Audio Calling

The app uses RECORD_AUDIO permission strictly for enabling live voice calling functionality.

  • We do not record your audio.
  • We do not store any voice data.
  • We do not listen in the background.
  • We do not share your audio with third parties.

The microphone is only used when you start and participate in a voice call. You may revoke microphone access anytime from your device settings.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide audio calling and app functionality.
  • To maintain security and prevent misuse.
  • To improve performance and fix issues.

5. Data Processing

Any audio handled during calls is processed in real-time only to enable communication and is not stored permanently.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only when required by law, or when necessary to protect user safety.

7. Data Retention
