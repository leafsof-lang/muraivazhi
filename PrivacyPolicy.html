3. Microphone Permission & Audio Usage

The app requests RECORD_AUDIO permission ONLY for enabling live audio calling features.

  • We do not record your voice.
  • We do not store audio.
  • We do not listen in the background.
  • We do not share audio with any third party.

The microphone is used strictly during an active call started by you. You may revoke microphone permission anytime from your device settings.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To improve app stability and performance.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties unless legally required.
