Muraivazhi — Data Policy

Last updated: October 28, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Muraivazhi, developed and managed by Leafsof Technologies. This Data Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how long we keep it, and how you can control or delete your data.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use the app:

  • Personal information: name, email address, phone number, gender, date of birth, and other details you provide.
  • User content: questions submitted for consultation, audio session details, and optional profile details.
  • Location: basic city/state if you choose to share it. We do not collect precise GPS unless explicitly required for a feature and enabled by you.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To schedule and provide astrology consultations.
  • To facilitate audio consultation and communication features.
  • To improve our service, perform analytics, and fix bugs.
  • To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Consulting astrologers only for providing your requested consultation.
  • Service providers who support operations (e.g., analytics, cloud hosting, notifications).
  • Legal or regulatory authorities when required by law.

5. Security

All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS to encrypt data in transit. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data, including access controls and encrypted backups where appropriate. However, no system is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

  • We retain personal data while your account is active.
  • If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, unless legally required to retain specific records (e.g., payment records).
  • Aggregated or anonymized analytics data may be retained.
How to delete your account
  1. Open the Muraivazhi app → Settings → Create a Ticket to Delete Account
  2. Send an email to leafsof@gmail.com with the subject “Delete my account” and include your registered phone number.

After your request, we will confirm by email and complete the removal within the stated timeframe.

7. User Controls

  • You can update or remove profile fields directly in the app.
  • You may contact support for consultation concerns or misuse.
  • To request a copy or deletion of your data, contact leafsof@gmail.com.

8. Children

Muraivazhi is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Data Policy periodically. When updated, we will revise the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes implies acceptance of the revised policy.
